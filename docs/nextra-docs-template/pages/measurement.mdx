# Measurement and Verification

## Accuracy Measurement

### Introduction
* **Purpose**: Describe the methods to measure the AI system's accuracy.
* **Scope**: Metrics used, benchmarks, and regularity of checks.

### Measurement Techniques
* Use of confusion matrix and ROC curves among others.
* Periodic accuracy checks against validation data.

## Alignment assessment

### Introduction
* **Purpose**: Detail the assessment of the AI system's alignment with business and ethical goals.
* **Scope**: Alignment metrics, stakeholder feedback, and adjustments.

### Assessment Methods
* Regular alignment checks with business objectives.
* Feedback collection on ethical considerations.
